Letter of Reference for Melanie Taylor

It is with great pleasure I write this letter of reference on behalf of attorney Melanie Taylor whom I have known for several years. As a colleague, I have witnessed Melanie exhibit a high standard of excellence in our legal profession. She is intelligent, hardworking, and resourceful often showcasing her keen analytical skills and her wonderful grasp of the law when representing her clients in the courtroom. Melanie is detail-oriented, thorough, and untiring in her career never taking the path of least resistance but often challenging herself for continue growth.

Not only is her professionalism beyond reproach, but so is her personality and character. She is a person of high moral standards, well respected by judges and attorneys, and held in high esteem by all who know her. Whenever colleagues have legal issues or questions, she does not hesitate to help often providing her wealth of knowledge, expertise, and resources. There has never been a time when I asked a question and she denied me. Although her schedule is full as a sole practitioner, a divorce referee, a mental health attorney, and a wife, she makes time to help a colleague in need. Finally, Melanie is a beacon of light for her community. She is an active member of Brown Baptist Church and is sponsor of the Young Actors Guild a nonprofit organization which exposes inner city youths to dance and theatrical productions.

For the foregoing reasons, I am honored to provide this letter of reference for Attorney Melanie Taylor.

Sincerely,
DeVenus Foster, Esq.
